Documentaries
Documentary Londres

The Work / London Masons

The documentary "The Work" follows Lee Townsend, a London Freemason, from his everyday life in Croydon to the grandeur of the Freemasons' Hall. With the grime song of the same name as its backdrop, this documentary does not attempt to explain Freemasonry, but rather to show our commitment to personal growth and community.

Filmed by Eddy Kirk, a London Freemason, and produced by London's communications manager, Bro. Omaid Hiwaizi, the documentary features several London Freemasons, reflecting the diversity and reality of the Order today.
