April 2026 Province

XVI Assembly of the Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia

The Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia held its XVI Assembly on 18 April 2026 at the Salones del Hotel Agalia in Murcia, with the participation of more than one hundred brethren, including members of the province's lodges and visitors from other Provincial Grand Lodges.

The Assembly was presided over by the Provincial Grand Master R∴W∴Bro. Luis Alcaina Guzmán, who directed the proceedings of the day. In the course of the meeting, the provincial officers were invested for the 2026–2027 term.

The Assembly took place in a spirit of fraternity and commitment to the values that define our obedience: Tradition, Fraternity and Service.

April 2026 Charity

Donation of €500 to FUENSOCIAL

The Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia donated €500 to FUENSOCIAL, an association supporting people with disabilities or at risk of developing them, and their families, through the Provincial Grand Lodge of Andalusia. A reflection of Murcian Freemasonry's fraternal commitment beyond provincial boundaries.

December 2025 Charity

The Province of Murcia donates €1,600 to AFACMUR

The Province of Murcia has donated the €1,600 raised at its Provincial Gala to AFACMUR, in support of their work helping children, young people and their families throughout the cancer treatment process.

The Provincial Grand Master Luis Alcaina and the Provincial Grand Secretary Ángel Sánchez made the presentation in person.

2022 Charity

Luz de Murcia No. 91 donates €1,000 in food to Los Alcázares

Luz de Murcia Lodge donated food items valued at €1,000 to the municipal social services to strengthen the local food bank's reserves.

In Murcia, nearly 30,000 people rely on food banks for their daily needs.

2022 Media

The Province on Nemesis Radio: 15th anniversary of the Provincial Grand Lodge

Representatives of the Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia took part in the Nemesis Radio programme to mark the Province's 15th anniversary.

Freemasonry, its values and its presence in the Region of Murcia were discussed, responding to questions from listeners.
