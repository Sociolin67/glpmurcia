Freemasons helping Murcia

Charity has always been a fundamental part of Freemasonry. Not as an occasional gesture, but as a permanent moral obligation: the commitment to give back to the community a part of what the community gives us.

All the lodges in the Province are actively involved in their local community. The Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia coordinates and promotes these initiatives, combining the collective effort of its 145 Freemasons spread across six regular lodges.

In Murcia, nearly 30,000 people rely on food banks to meet their basic daily needs — a figure that has continued to grow. Faced with this reality, the Masonic response is concrete, local and sustained over time.

"Charity is at the very heart of Freemasonry."

Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia

Between March and July 2022, 850,000 kilograms of basic foodstuffs were distributed in the Region of Murcia — an increase of almost 40% on the previous year.

How we act
◈

Food banks

The Province's lodges work directly with municipal social services to strengthen the reserves of local food banks, especially during periods of greatest demand.

✦

Charity dinners

The Province's Annual Gala Dinner includes charitable activities — raffles and collections — whose proceeds go entirely to regional associations selected for their social impact.

◇

Support for associations

The Province directly supports organisations such as MABS Murcia and Proyecto Oblatas, which work with vulnerable communities in our region.

⬡

Provincial initiative

The Provincial Grand Master coordinates province-wide initiatives that combine the efforts of all the lodges, multiplying the impact of each individual action.

Recent actions

December 2025

Provincial Gala: €1,600 for AFACMUR

The Province of Murcia donated the €1,600 raised at its Provincial Gala to AFACMUR, an association dedicated to supporting children, young people and their families throughout the cancer treatment process. The Provincial Grand Master Luis Alcaina and Provincial Grand Secretary Ángel Sánchez made the presentation in person.

March 2023

Gala Dinner: €1,000 for MABS Murcia and Proyecto Oblatas

At the Province's Annual Gala Dinner, €1,000 was raised through a raffle, distributed equally between MABS Murcia and Proyecto Oblatas. The Provincial Grand Master, R.W.Bro. Terence Pierce, personally presented the cheques to representatives of both organisations.

2022

Luz de Murcia No. 91: €1,000 in food to Los Alcázares

Luz de Murcia Lodge donated food items valued at €1,000 to the social services of Los Alcázares. The donation was received by Javier Pacheco, head of Municipal Social Services, in the presence of lodge members and the Provincial Grand Master.

Ongoing initiative

Ongoing support for the region's food banks

The Province maintains an active and sustained collaboration with the food banks of the Region of Murcia, responding to the needs of the nearly 30,000 people who depend on them in our community.
