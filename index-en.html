Welcome from the Provincial Grand Master

"It is an honour to welcome you to the Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia."

R∴W∴Bro∴ Luis Alcaina Guzmán — Provincial Grand Master of Murcia

It is an honour to welcome you to the website of the Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia. We are part of the only internationally recognised regular Freemasonry and the largest in all of Spain.

This space is a meeting point for Masons and non-initiates alike. To those not yet initiated, we invite you to explore the values and principles that guide our Order. We are at your disposal for any enquiry.

May the light of the Great Architect of the Universe illuminate our path. Welcome to the Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia! Fraternally.

R∴W∴Bro∴ Luis Alcaina Guzmán · Provincial Grand Master of Murcia

Tradition, Fraternity and Service

The Provincial Grand Lodge of Murcia, a constitutional part of the Grand Lodge of Spain, unites seven Regular Lodges committed to the values of universal fraternity, moral progress and service to the community.
